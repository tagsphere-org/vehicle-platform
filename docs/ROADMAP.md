# TagSphere – Product Vision & Expansion Roadmap

## 1. Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** TagSphere
**Domain:** tagsphere.co.in

**Purpose:**
TagSphere is a smart identity and QR-based solutions platform designed to connect people, vehicles, assets, and living beings through secure digital tags.

This document serves as a long-term roadmap to build multiple products under one umbrella brand. New products can be added gradually over time.

---

## 2. Core Vision

To build a unified ecosystem where a simple QR/NFC tag can help:

- Contact the right person
- Identify ownership
- Provide emergency information
- Improve safety
- Enable smart communication

TagSphere should grow from a single product into a multi-category smart tag company.

---

## 3. Phase-wise Product Expansion Plan

### Phase 1 – Vehicle Solutions (MVP Focus)

Initial product to validate business idea and generate revenue.

**Products:**

1. Car contact QR sticker
2. Bike contact QR sticker
3. Parking alert tag
4. Fleet vehicle identity tag

**Core features:**

- Scan QR → Contact owner
- Owner registration dashboard
- OTP-based linking
- Call/alert button

**Goal:**

- Validate demand
- Build initial customer base
- Generate first revenue stream

---

### Phase 2 – Pet Safety Solutions (After Validation)

**Products:**

- Urban pet owners
